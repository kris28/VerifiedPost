About LASHSTL

Browse Mobile app has hundreds of different barbers, lash techs, and eyebrow technicians and even lawn care . When you open the app, you can scroll through the feed for inspiration or search for a specific technician . When you find something you like, tap to add it to your cart . o.

Order When you’re ready to check out, you’ll see your address, an estimated arrival time, and the price of the order including tax and booking fee along with the time the desired order work time. When everything looks right, just tap Place order–and that’s it. We’ll automatically use the card on file so you never need cash.

Track Follow your Tech in the app. First you’ll see the Technician to accept and start prepping. Then, a nearby Mobile Looks Partner–in a car will drive or ride to you. You’ll be able to see their name and photo and track progress on the map.
